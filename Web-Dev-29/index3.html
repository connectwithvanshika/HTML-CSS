V A N S H I K A

  • Inline Elements:

    Do not start on a new line and stay within the flow of the text. Occupy only as much width as their content. Cannot have height, width, or top/bottom margins applied directly. Examples: span, a, strong, img.

  • Block Elements:

    Start on a new line and take up the full width of their container. Allow height, width, padding, and margin to be applied. Examples: div, p, h1 h6, section
